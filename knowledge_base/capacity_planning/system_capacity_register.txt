===========================
SHEET 4 — CAPACITY RECOMMENDATIONS
===========================

PRIORITY 1 — PAYMENTS-GW Scale-Up (Immediate)
  Issue: Peak CPU 88%, approaching 500 TPS ceiling. Dec 2024 near-breach event.
  Recommendation: Add second PAYMENTS-GW instance (horizontal scale) behind load balancer.
  Estimated effort: 2 sprints. Infra cost: ~$1,200/month additional compute.
  Risk if not done: Service degradation during next peak period (Dec 2025 or major promotional event).

PRIORITY 2 — FRAUD-ENGINE Optimisation (Q3 2025)
  Issue: Peak CPU 94%, highest across all services. Redis cache TTL reduction increases recompute load.
  Recommendation: Profile scoring model inference. Consider model quantisation or GPU offload for batch scoring.
  Note: Quarterly retraining (FRAUD-RETRAIN-001) spikes CPU to 100% for ~3 hours — schedule during low-traffic window.

PRIORITY 3 — ELK Node Expansion (Q3 2025)
  Issue: RAM peak 91%, hot-tier disk filling at rate of ~180 GB/month.
  Recommendation: Add second ELK node (current single-node setup is also a resilience risk).
  Estimated effort: 1 sprint. Storage cost: +$400/month.

PRIORITY 4 — CBS Oracle DB Storage Planning (Q4 2025)
  Issue: At current growth rate, hits 80% capacity by May 2026.
  Recommendation: DBA team to review table partitioning and archival strategy in Q3 2025.
  Archival candidate: Transaction records >3 years old (~600 GB eligible).
