===========================
REGULATORY FRAMEWORKS
===========================

PCI-DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard)
  Scope: CBS, PAYMENTS-GW, SWIFT-ADAPTER
  Requirements:
    - No storage of CVV/CVC data after authorisation
    - Cardholder data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.2+)
    - Access restricted to need-to-know (RBAC enforced)
    - CBS_SESSION_TIMEOUT_SEC minimum 900 seconds (PCI-DSS Req 8.6)
  Annual assessment: Qualified Security Assessor (QSA) audit in Q3

GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  Scope: All systems handling EU customer personal data
  Requirements:
    - Data minimisation
    - Right to erasure: fulfilled within 30 days (submit ticket to Data Engineering)
    - Data transfers outside EU require Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
    - Breach notification to regulators within 72 hours
  DPO: dpo@company.com

Basel III / Central Bank Reporting
  Scope: REPORTING-SVC, DWH
  Requirements:
    - Liquidity Coverage Ratio (LCR): daily (RPT-001 job)
    - Net Stable Funding Ratio (NSFR): monthly
    - Submit to regulator within 1 business day of generation

===========================
DATA CLASSIFICATION
===========================

PUBLIC: No restriction. Marketing materials, public rate sheets.
INTERNAL: Internal use only. Process docs, architecture diagrams. No external sharing.
CONFIDENTIAL: Customer accounts, transactions, financial reports. Encrypted. RBAC enforced.
RESTRICTED: Cardholder PAN, SWIFT keys, credentials, fraud model details.
  - HSM-managed encryption keys. CISO approval for access. Immutable audit log.

===========================
DATA RETENTION
===========================

Transaction Records: 7 years (PCI-DSS + banking regulation)
Customer KYC Documents: 5 years after account closure (AML)
SWIFT Message Logs: 10 years
Authentication Logs: 7 years
Application Logs (non-compliance): 1 year
Fraud Model Training Data: 3 years

===========================
GDPR ERASURE PROCESS
===========================

1. Customer submits erasure request via portal or in writing.
2. DPO verifies identity and eligibility (no legal hold).
3. Data Engineering executes deletion across CBS, DWH, backups within 30 days.
4. Deletion confirmed in writing to customer.
Note: Records under regulatory retention cannot be deleted. Customer is notified.
